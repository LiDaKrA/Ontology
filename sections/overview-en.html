2. : Overview back to ToC

The OntoFuhSen vocabulary models the main concepts for FuhSen (Federated Hybrid Search Engine). FuhSen search integrates and summarizes information about entities that are spread across the Web. It takes advantages of the API's search facilities that the different data sources provide (e.g. Facebook, Twitter, etc).
